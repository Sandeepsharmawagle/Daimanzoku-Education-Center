About Daimanzoku Education Center

Established: December 5th, 2024

Daimanzoku Education Center Pvt. Ltd. is a legally registered educational consultancy providing professional support exclusively for students aspiring to study and work in Japan. We specialize in Japanese language education and career facilitation.

Our Objectives

  • Assist students in choosing the right universities and courses in Japan
  • Facilitate smooth and effective visa application processes for Japan
  • Enhance Japanese language proficiency through comprehensive test preparation
  • Prepare students mentally, socially, and behaviorally for life in Japan
  • Maintain transparency between students, guardians, and consultancy
